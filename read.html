What is Astronomy?

Astronomy is the study of everything in space. We study all different kinds of objects that are beyond our planet earth including other planets, the moon, just about anything we can see in the sky and can’t see!  We study these objects using special instruments and tools such as telescopes which help astronomers study these objects.
